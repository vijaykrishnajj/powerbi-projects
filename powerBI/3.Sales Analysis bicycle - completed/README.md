🚀 Project Overview

This project showcases an interactive Power BI dashboard developed to analyze the sales performance of a bicycle shop. It provides comprehensive insights into revenue, profit, product performance, and customer behavior, enabling informed and data-driven decision-making.

🎯 Objectives

Monitor overall business performance through key metrics

Analyze sales trends over time (monthly & yearly)

Identify top-performing products and customers

Understand regional and segment-wise sales distribution

📊 Key Features

📈 Interactive dashboard with year and month slicers

🎯 KPI cards for quick performance tracking

🌍 Geographic analysis of sales by country

🧩 Drill-down and filtering for detailed exploration

🎨 Clean and intuitive UI for better user experience

📌 Key KPIs

💰 Total Sales: 33.58M

📊 Total Profit: 4.86M

📦 Unit Sales: 318K

🏆 Top Product: Paseo

📊 Dashboard Highlights

📅 Monthly and yearly sales & profit trend analysis

🌍 Sales distribution by country and region

🛍️ Product-wise performance using donut & pie charts

👥 Customer-level analysis to identify top customers

🌳 Treemap visualization for business segment contribution

🔍 Insights Generated

Identified high-performing products and key revenue drivers

Discovered top customers contributing to overall sales

Analyzed seasonal trends and growth patterns

Highlighted region-wise performance differences

🛠️ Tools & Technologies

Power BI Desktop

Imported and connected sales data into Power BI

Designed interactive dashboards with visuals and slicers

Structured layout for easy navigation

Focus on clarity, usability, and storytelling

💡 Business Impact

Helps track business growth and performance trends
