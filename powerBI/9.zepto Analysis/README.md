🚀 Overview

This project showcases an interactive Power BI dashboard developed to analyze Zepto’s sales performance. It provides actionable insights into revenue, product performance, stock levels, and discount impact, enabling data-driven decisions for retail and e-commerce operations.

🎯 Objectives

Monitor overall revenue, sales volume, and average pricing metrics

Analyze category-wise performance and contribution

Track stock availability and impact of discounts

Identify top-selling products for inventory and marketing focus

Enable strategic decision-making in pricing and product management

📊 Key Features

📈 Interactive dashboards with dynamic filters and slicers

🎯 KPI cards for total revenue, products sold, and average pricing

📊 Category-wise analysis of sales quantity and revenue

🧾 Stock and discount impact insights

🎨 Clean, intuitive, and user-friendly dashboard design

📌 Key Metrics

💰 Total Revenue: 12 bn

🛍️ Products Sold: 796 K

🏷️ Average Selling Price

📊 Category-wise Sales & Revenue (e.g., Munchies, Chocolates, Cooking Essentials)

🏆 Top-Selling Products by Quantity

📊 Dashboard Insights

Bar charts, pie charts, and line graphs to visualize trends and distribution

Identification of out-of-stock items by category

Analysis of discounts’ impact on selling price

Comparison of top products and category contribution

🔍 Key Findings

Highlighted best-selling categories and products

Evaluated stock and inventory gaps for better management

Assessed discount strategies and pricing impact

Revealed trends in product demand and sales distribution
