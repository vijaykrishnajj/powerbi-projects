🚀 Overview

This project presents an end-to-end Power BI dashboard designed to analyze the impact of food habits on the health of people in India. It provides deep insights into dietary patterns, lifestyle factors, and health outcomes, enabling data-driven understanding of public health trends.

🎯 Objectives

Analyze the relationship between food habits and health outcomes

Evaluate health score distribution across demographics

Identify key lifestyle factors affecting health conditions

Compare dietary patterns and their impact on overall well-being

📊 Key Features

📈 Interactive dashboards with dynamic filters and slicers

🎯 KPI-driven insights into health performance metrics

📊 Correlation analysis using scatter plots and trend visuals

🗺️ Regional analysis of health trends across India

🎨 Clean, intuitive, and user-friendly dashboard design

📌 Key Analysis Areas

🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Health score distribution by age, gender, and demographics

⚖️ Relationship between BMI, calorie intake, and health score

🍽️ Comparison of diet types (Vegetarian, Non-Vegetarian, Vegan)

🧂 Impact of sugar and salt intake on health

🌍 Regional variations in health scores across India

🔍 Insights & Findings

Identified health trends across different demographic groups

Discovered correlations between dietary habits and health scores

Highlighted the impact of lifestyle factors on conditions like obesity, diabetes, and hypertension

Compared effectiveness of different diet types on overall health
