🚀 Overview

This project showcases a comprehensive Power BI dashboard developed to monitor and analyze financial performance. It provides detailed insights into revenue trends, regional performance, product distribution, and transactional data, enabling strategic and data-driven decision-making.

🎯 Objectives

Track overall revenue and average revenue performance

Analyze revenue distribution across regions and demographics

Evaluate product and store-level performance

Provide insights for financial planning and strategy

📊 Key Features

📈 Interactive dashboards with dynamic filters and slicers

🎯 KPI cards for real-time financial tracking

🌍 Regional and country-level performance analysis

🏪 Store-wise and product-wise revenue insights

🎨 Clean, intuitive, and user-friendly dashboard design

📌 Key Metrics

💰 Total Revenue

📊 Average Revenue

🌍 Revenue by Region (Asia, U.K., U.S.A.)

👥 Revenue by Gender

🏪 Store-wise Revenue Ranking (Store 1 – Store 5)

📊 Dashboard Insights

🌍 Regional revenue comparison across Asia, U.K., and U.S.A.

👥 Gender-based revenue distribution analysis

🛍️ Product-wise performance and contribution

🏪 Store ranking based on revenue generation

📅 Revenue trends across countries and time periods

🧾 Transaction-level and country-level summaries

🔍 Key Findings

Identified top-performing regions and countries

Highlighted high-revenue generating stores and products

Discovered patterns in customer demographics and revenue contribution

Provided insights into financial trends and growth opportunities
